Harvard-Radcliffe Modern Dance Company

Graphic Design, Spring & Fall 2016
Overview

I became the designer for the Harvard-Radcliffe Modern Dance Company (HRMDC) last winter, after dancing and choreographing for the company since my freshman fall. My first show we did as designer was Exposure, which played off the relationship between dance, photography, and video. Our design theme has generally been very minimalistic and contemporary, so I worked off the silhouette of a lens as the basis for our show logo, and then incorporated it into our other promotional materials like poster, cover photos, and programs. This semester we are celebrating our 40th anniversary with a show simply titled 40. I am working on developing a design theme for the show that brings together the concepts of past shows while moving forward to create a new brand identity for the company.
